An Everlasting Feud

Todays Top Story

1300

It's no secret the Montagues and Capulets violently hate each other, but the prince whishes they would make better efforts on keeping their "violent situations" out of the way of the public. Yesterday He showed he was not kidding.

A fight broke out after two parties were "biting thumbs" at each other, which eventually spread to the streets, and rakeing in anyone who sympathized or was apart of one of the two families, making the fight bigger, and more intense. The Death Toll is unknown, but officials don't doubt that many people were hurt in the fight. The prince argues that disturbances like this are a danger to the public, and defy his intended "peaceful way of life". No word on arrests, but the prince stated many shall be punished for their crimes.

Even still though the families argue over who started the fight, and most suggestions from those families have been ruled out, for the fear of them potentially being biased. Many suggestions are unclear as well, as the families refuse to come to a decision, or speak clearly, and blame keeps getting passed back and forth.
